Department of Information Systems

The Information System Department of Faculty of Computer and Information of Assiut University established since the establishment of the faculty according to the Presidential Decree No. (292) for the year 2001 to prepare graduates supplied with specialized scientific knowledge assets in the field of information systems to keep pace with the changes programs of the modern department and is also used the possibilities available to solve the problems of society. This is done through the presenting excellent curricula in the various branches of information systems, scientific research and applied subject to international standards, quality and positive interaction of the role of the department in the service of the environment and community development.

Department Vision

Information Systems Department seeks to provide elements of the ongoing development of education in the field of information systems to keep pace with rapid scientific and technological university and improve its performance, and raise graduates of the department to the level of excellence, high competitiveness and the completion of research and scientific studies, and to great the role of the department in the environment services and community development.

Department Objectives

1 - Preparation of specialists in the field of information systems and management of data and secure qualified with theoretical assets and applications to enable them to global competition in the development of computer technology and information .
2 - Conducting studies and scientific and applied research in the field of information systems that have a direct impact on the integral development of society.
3 - Provide scientific and technical advice and assistance for bodies and agencies that use the techniques of information systems and components concerned with the technology .
4 - Raising the level of graduates of different faculties, according to labor market needs in the fields of modern information systems by holding applied diplomas .
5 - Participation with specialized agencies to develop and arabization of software information systems and networks and applications .
6 - Organization of seminars and scientific conferences, with a view to deepening the concepts and upgrading scientific cooperation between the specialized cadres .
7 - A scientific agreements with corresponding bodies and institutions at the local, regional and global in order to exchange experiences and research on the disciplines of information systems .
8 - proposal for the establishment of a special unite of the specialized and advanced research in the field of information systems .
